DC Films

The list of films adapted from DC Comics dates back to 1951 with the movie, Superman and the Mole Men.

Over the course of the 65+ year history of tv and film adaptations, DC has stuck to developing its two most popular characters, Batman and Superman. In fact their highest grossing film, by no surprise, is The Dark Knight Rises, amassing $1.085 billion!



Batman vs. Superman: Dawn of Justice

The battle between them has dated back decades to the Silver Age of Comics (mid-1950's), and the predominate theme is that Batman takes preemptive steps to make sure Superman and the other super-powered heroes of Justice League can be stopped in case they lose control.
